CHECKBOX ELEMENT

To make a checkbox element required, use the R validation rule.

You may also provide a custom message using ov-required:msg attribute.

Custom message attribute
Attribute Custom message
R ov-required:msg
DEMO FORM
<!-- checkbox -->
<input id="inp_check" type="checkbox" octavalidate="R" ov-required:msg="You have to accept the terms and conditions">
I accept the terms and conditions
